About us

Kang and Associates

Established in 1979, Kang and Associates is a premier Organsiation based in New Delhi, India, renowned for its comprehensive legal expertise and international acumen. Under the leadership of Mr. TPS Kang and supervised by Ms. Upasana Kang, our Organsiation has earned a distinguished reputation for providing high-quality legal advisory services to corporations, individuals, and international entities worldwide.

Since its inception in the year 1979, Kang and Associates has been undergoing dynamic progression, expanding its breadth of services in India and abroad. We have strategic alliances with many other Organsiations and lawyers spreading all over India and abroad to provide seamless service to a multitude of clients.


At Kang and Associates, providing workable advice and cost effective assistance to our clients is our foremost aim. The clients benefit from our approach of viewing issues from both a business as well as a legal perspective; focusing on problem avoidance, not just problem solving. With a sophisticated understanding of changing business environment intersecting with law, public policy and technology, the Organsiation is positioned to protect and advance its clients’ Business Interests in the present era of Globalization.

Our Goal

At Kang and Associates, our primary goal is to deliver insightful, effective, and cost-efficient legal solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client. We aim to view issues from both business and legal perspectives, focusing on proactive problem avoidance rather than mere problem solving. Our approach ensures that clients benefit from our sophisticated understanding of the intersection between law, public policy, technology, and the dynamic business environment of the global era.

Our Ethos

Our ethos is built on a foundation of integrity, excellence, and client-centric service. We are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of service and ethics while maintaining a positive outlook in all our engagements. Over the decades, our commitment to quality and professionalism has made us the counsel of choice for a diverse range of legal matters, both domestically and internationally.

Our Aim

Our aim is to be at the forefront of legal practice, continuously expanding and evolving to meet the demands of a rapidly changing global landscape. Through our strategic alliances with Organsiations and lawyers across India and abroad, we provide seamless, integrated legal services to a broad spectrum of clients. Whether navigating complex arbitration proceedings, defending against economic offenses, handling finance and securities litigation, or addressing any other legal challenges, we are dedicated to protecting and advancing our clients’ interests with unwavering dedication and expertise.

Practice Area

Home Practice Area
Domestic & International Arbitrations

Our Organsiation is adept at handling arbitration proceedings, both within India and internationally. We guide our clients through the entire arbitration process, from initial filings to final awards, ensuring their interests are effectively represented. We deal specially in Arbitrations involved in Construction, Real Estate Commercial Contracts and company disputes.

Economic Offences

We provide comprehensive legal defense for clients charged with economic offences such as fraud and financial mismanagement. Our team is skilled in navigating the complexities of economic crime investigations and court proceedings, aiming to secure favorable outcomes through diligent case preparation and strategic defense.

Finance and Securities Litigation

In the realm of finance and securities, we address legal issues related to investments, trading, and regulatory compliance. Our Organsiation represents clients in disputes involving stock market transactions, securities fraud, and regulatory violations, leveraging our deep understanding of financial regulations to protect our clients' interests.

Insurance Disputes

We handle disputes involving insurance policies, including coverage issues and claims denials. Our goal is to resolve conflicts between policyholders and insurers by thoroughly examining policy terms, negotiating settlements, and, if necessary, representing clients in court to achieve fair and just outcomes.

Labour Litigation

Our practice in labour litigation encompasses disputes between employers and employees, such as wrongful dismissal, wage disputes, and workplace harassment. We work to resolve these issues efficiently while ensuring compliance with labour laws and protecting the rights of all parties involved. We provide all round support to our clients right from the Labour office to the Labour courts.

Litigation in Administrative Courts where Government is Involved

We represent clients in matters involving administrative actions by government bodies. This includes challenges to government decisions, regulatory enforcement, and public law disputes. Our Organsiation is experienced in navigating the complexities of administrative law to effectively advocate for our clients.

Real Estate Litigation

We address legal disputes related to real estate transactions, including issues with property titles, lease agreements, and development projects. Our team provides expert advice and representation to resolve conflicts involving property ownership, boundaries, and contractual obligations.

Recovery Proceedings

Our Organsiation assists clients in recovering debts through legal action. Whether dealing with unpaid invoices or enforcing judgments, we pursue effective recovery strategies, including court proceedings and settlement negotiations, to help clients reclaim owed amounts.

Taxation Related Litigation

We offer legal support in disputes related to taxation, including issues with tax assessments, refunds, and compliance with tax regulations. Our aim is to resolve these matters efficiently, ensuring clients adhere to tax laws while minimizing their financial liabilities.

Customs and Excise

We represent clients in matters related to customs and excise duties, including disputes over tariff classifications, valuation, and compliance with import-export regulations. Our expertise helps clients navigate the complexities of international trade regulations and resolve issues with customs authorities.

Matrimonial Disputes

Our Organsiation handles family law matters with sensitivity and professionalism, including divorce, child custody, and property division. We aim to resolve matrimonial disputes in a way that addresses the legal and emotional aspects, working towards fair and amicable solutions.

Banking

We address a wide range of banking-related issues, including disputes over loans, banking regulations, and financial transactions. Our Organsiation represents clients in conflicts with banks and financial institutions, striving to protect their rights and achieve favorable resolutions.

Consumer Protection

We advocate for consumers in cases involving defective products, poor services, and unfair trade practices. Our goal is to ensure that consumers receive compensation for grievances and that their rights are upheld in accordance with consumer protection laws.

Civil Litigation

Our practice covers various civil disputes, from contract breaches and property issues to personal injury claims. We provide legal representation in court and alternative dispute resolution forums, working to achieve fair outcomes through effective advocacy and negotiation.

Criminal Litigation

We offer defense services for clients facing criminal charges, ranging from minor offenses to serious crimes. Our Organsiation’s approach includes a thorough investigation of the case, strategic defense planning, and courtroom representation to safeguard our clients’ rights and interests.

Intellectual Property Laws Our Organsiation specializes in protecting and enforcing intellectual property rights, including patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets. We assist clients in safeguarding their innovations and creative works through legal actions and negotiations to prevent infringement and unauthorized use.
